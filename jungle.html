4-Day Jungle Adventure Expedition

Embark on an exciting journey through the lush jungles of Uttarakhand!

Day 1: Arrival and Jungle Camp Setup

Arrive at the jungle base camp and get settled in your tents. Learn basic survival skills from expert guides and participate in setting up the jungle camp.

Day 2: Jungle Trekking and Bird Watching

Embark on an exhilarating jungle trek through dense forests, spotting exotic wildlife and colorful birds along the way. Learn about the flora and fauna of the jungle ecosystem.

Day 3: Waterfall Exploration and River Rafting

Discover hidden waterfalls nestled deep within the jungle and take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear waters. Experience the thrill of river rafting through the rapids of the jungle river.

Day 4: Canopy Walk and Departure

Embark on a thrilling canopy walk high above the jungle floor, offering panoramic views of the surrounding wilderness. Bid farewell to the jungle as you head back home with unforgettable memories.
