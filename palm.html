3-Day Trip to Palm Beach

Experience the beauty of Palm Beach in just three days!

Day 1: Arrival and Beach Relaxation

Upon arrival in Palm Beach, check in to your beachfront hotel. Spend the afternoon relaxing on the stunning white sandy beaches and enjoying the warm ocean breeze.

Day 2: Explore Palm Beach

Visit the famous Worth Avenue for upscale shopping and dining. Explore the iconic Breakers Hotel and Museum. Don't forget to take a leisurely stroll through the picturesque botanical gardens.

Day 3: Water Activities and Departure

Spend your last day trying out water activities like snorkeling or paddleboarding. Enjoy a delicious seafood lunch by the marina before departing with unforgettable memories of Palm Beach.
