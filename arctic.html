4-Day Arctic Wilderness Expedition

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the Arctic wilderness!

Day 1: Arrival and Northern Lights Tour

Upon arrival, settle into your cozy Arctic lodge. In the evening, embark on a guided tour to witness the mesmerizing Northern Lights dancing across the Arctic sky.

Day 2: Dog Sledding Adventure

Experience the thrill of dog sledding through the snow-covered wilderness. Learn about the fascinating history of dog sledding and interact with the friendly huskies.

Day 3: Ice Fishing and Snowshoeing

Join a local guide for an ice fishing excursion on a frozen lake. Afterwards, strap on snowshoes and explore the serene Arctic landscape, taking in breathtaking views of snow-capped mountains.

Day 4: Wildlife Safari and Departure

Embark on a wildlife safari to spot Arctic wildlife such as reindeer, Arctic foxes, and maybe even polar bears! Bid farewell to the Arctic wilderness as you head back home with unforgettable memories.
